REVIEWS

David Horn, DOA Zine #9:
ASHES OF MANKIND
A SCENE IN HELL
LOST APPARITIONS RECORDS
8 SKULLS
Encylopedia Metallum describes ASHES OF MANKIND as a Melodic Death Metal band but once the skin on your face is burned to a charred husk by the brutal assault of A Scene in Hell you will find yourself wondering "where is the fucking melody?" This Baltimore based death squad consisting of the three As (Alex, Ashton & Armon) have crafted a tasty little treat for fans of churning Death/Thrash Metal with A Scene in Hell as tracks like "Show of Arms", "Raise the Dead" and "Tides of War" blast forth with considerable power and conviction and rarely fall into repetition. True underground artwork and packaging with "Visions of Hell" images and a great logo courtesy of Luciana Nedelea help make this one of my favorite Lost Apparitions releases to date. (DH)
